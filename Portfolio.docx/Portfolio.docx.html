I am an Electronics And Communication Engineering graduate from the National Institute Of Technology Calicut. I have a wide range of experience working in the industry and academia. Below I have attached three projects of mine:

  • Depth estimation on an RGB image.

Synthetic Dataset Generation

Created datasets in Unity3D along with their labels. Under different constraints, the environment was allowed to generate new samples randomly.

Hand Pose Data

Generated different poses of hands and landmarks on randomly generated poses were determined using a unity function camera.WorldToScreenPoint and used that as the label for my project.

 Depth Data

Similar to the above project random objects were generated in a scene with different sizes, colours and orientations along with dynamically changing environmental conditions.

Labels for the data was also generated by calculating the distance of each object from the camera and labelling darker shade for purposes for nearer objects and light for faraway.

 

Depth Perception Using RGB images

June 2018

Extraction depth data from RGB images were experimented using a GAN(Generative Adversarial Network). Used the above generated depth dataset for the model.
